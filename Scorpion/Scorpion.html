    Hanzo Hasashi's Story.                        
    
    In a sudden attack, the Lin Kuei ninja clan invades 
    its rival clan Shirai Ryu and slaughters everyone,
    including the wife of grandmaster Hanzo Hasashi. 
    When Hasashi and his son, Satoshi return from the jungle,
    they find their clan dead at night. 
    Lin Kuei’s men attack Hasashi and his son but he kills them all when
    suddenly the grandmaster of Lin Kuei,
    Sub-Zero freezes Hasashi and mercilessly slaughters him and his young son.
    Hasashi wakes up in chains in the depths of hell called Netherrealm.
    Hasashi was mysteriously saved and resurrected by the sorcerer Quan Chi.
    Quan Chi wants Hasashi to attend the Mortal Kombat tournament 
    organized by Shang Tsung, and steal a key from his temple.
    The key will unlock the prison that has locked
    Quan Chi’s master, Shinnok, for a long time.
    In return for his favor, Quan Chi promises that his master has the power
    to bring back Hasashi’s slaughtered family 
    and even send him back to the mortal realm. 
    He also lures Hasashi by informing him that his rival 
    Sub-Zero will be attending the tournament. 
    Hasashi, burning with the fire of vengeance 
    transforms himself into Scorpion and joins the tournament.                  
    The protector of the Earth realm, Lord Raiden has forged
    a team of fighters to fight for the Earth.
    These fighters include Shaolin monk Liu Kang, 
    Hollywood actor Johnny Cage and Special Forces agent Sonya Blade.                   
    The runners-up of the Mortal Kombat tournament will fight against Shang Tsung’s champion Goro.
    The winner will decide the fate of the Earth Realm.

    Eventually Scorpion encounters Sub-Zero. 
    At this point Scorpion still thinks that the Lin Kuei clan and Sub-Zero killed his clan and family.
    To take his revenge from Sub-Zero, Scorpion jumps off on a spike pit that kills both of them. 
    Quan Chi is angered by Scorpion’s move to kill himself and he curses him. 
    He also informs him that it was Quan Chi who disguised as Sub-Zero and slaughtered Hasashi’s clan and wife, 
    so as to persuade him to fight for him. But in the end, he was useless.
    
    Quan Shi reveals the truth about the death of Hanzo's family.

    In the last battle of the tournament, Liu Kang is struggling against Goro.
    He was about to be ripped apart when Scorpion saves his soul.
    Scorpion kills Goro by pulling out his head from the spine.
    Scorpion demands Quan Chi who is trapped in Shang Tsung’s inferno for poisoning Shang’s drink.
    Shang Tsung offers Quan Chi and Earth realm to Scorpion if he wins the tournament and for that,
    he has to fight against Liu Kang. Scorpion decides otherwise, and takes Shang Tsung hostage,
    surrendering the fight to Liu Kang. Lord Raiden declared Liu Kang the winner and the earth realm is protected.
    The temple, however, starts trembling and fighters make their move out of it.
    In a massive battle, Scorpion avenges his family by killing Quan Chi. 
    Shao Kahn is enraged with Outerworld’s defeat and prepares for battle against the Earth Realm.
    He pledges to end the game, once and for all. Lord Raiden consoles a disappointed Liu Kang,
    that he wasn’t destined to win against Goro but against Shao Kahn.
